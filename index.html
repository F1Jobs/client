FAQs


1. What is the purpose of this site?

If you are looking for a job at one of the Formula 1 teams, browsing their individual pages for job listings gets tedious. So I made this site to display it all in one place, and apply easily. I've seen similar sites exist, but either they are not updated frequently, or diverge too far from the current teams.


2. How frequently does it get updated?

Any update to job listings at original sites gets updated here within 30 minutes. Job listings don't change dramatically in a short period of time, so hitting the original sites for instantaneous updates would only make the site slower.


3. Can I see the source?

Sure, the website is open-source under the GNU General Public License v3.0. The source code is hosted on GitHub.
